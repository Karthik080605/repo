Famous Tourist Places

Explore the most beautiful places around the world.

Eiffel Tower, Paris

The Eiffel Tower is one of the most recognizable structures in the world, located in Paris, France.

Great Wall of China.

The Great Wall of China is a series of fortifications made of stone, brick, tamped earth, wood, and other materials.

Taj Mahal, India

The Taj Mahal is an ivory-white marble mausoleum on the south bank of the Yamuna river in the Indian city of Agra.

Grand Canyon, USA

The Grand Canyon is a steep-sided canyon carved by the Colorado River in Arizona, United States.

Sydney Opera House, Australia

The Sydney Opera House is a multi-venue performing arts centre in Sydney, Australia.

Colosseum, Rome

The Colosseum is an oval amphitheatre in the centre of the city of Rome, Italy.

Statue of Liberty, USA

The Statue of Liberty is a colossal neoclassical sculpture on Liberty Island in New York Harbor in New York City, in the United States.

Christ the Redeemer, Brazil

Christ the Redeemer is an Art Deco statue of Jesus Christ in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil.

Machu Picchu, Peru

Machu Picchu is a 15th-century Inca citadel located in the Eastern Cordillera of southern Peru.
